About samoonEvents

Born in Dubai, Connecting the World

Our Story

Founded in the vibrant city of Dubai in 2025, samoonEvents was born from a vision to transform how people plan and manage their special moments. Inspired by the city's blend of tradition and innovation, we've created a platform that combines cutting-edge technology with personalized service.

This journey began after recognizing the need for a comprehensive solution that simplifies the complexities of event planning while preserving the joy of creating memorable experiences.

Our Mission

At samoonEvents, we're on a mission to make event planning accessible, enjoyable, and stress-free for everyone, anywhere in the world. We believe every celebration deserves to be extraordinary, whether it's an intimate gathering or a grand wedding.

We strive to empower our users with intuitive tools and resources that bring their vision to life, while saving them time and reducing complexity. By connecting event hosts with quality vendors and simplifying guest management, we help create seamless experiences from concept to celebration.

Our Global Presence

From Dubai to the World

While our roots are firmly planted in Dubai, our platform serves event planners across six continents. We've helped create memorable experiences in over 50 countries, bringing our unique blend of Middle Eastern hospitality and technological innovation to the global stage.

Our Core Values

Excellence

We're committed to delivering exceptional quality in everything we do, from our platform's functionality to customer support.

Global Perspective

We embrace cultural diversity and design our platform to serve event planners from all backgrounds and traditions.

Innovation

User-Centered
